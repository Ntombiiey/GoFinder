GoFinder is a web-based platform that helps users discover local spots in urban areas like Pretoria. It caters to people who enjoy dining, socializing, or exploring by offering detailed information about businesses, such as Wi-Fi availability, pet-friendliness, and user reviews. The platform promotes lesser-known local businesses by providing personalized recommendations and features an easy-to-use interface with advanced filters for customized searches. GoFinder aims to boost user engagement and improve visibility for local businesses.
