About GoFinder

GoFinder simplifies the process of discovering local activities, restaurants, and relaxing spots. Finding activities or relaxing spots in a huge area such as Pretoria can be challenging and time-consuming. It involves visiting multiple locations to compare prices, check for specials, or ensure proper Wi-Fi connectivity for work purposes. We aim to simplify this process by helping users find such locations near them, partnering with local businesses that are not easily found or well-known.
